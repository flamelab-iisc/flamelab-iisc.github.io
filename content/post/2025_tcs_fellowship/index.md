Shiva has been awarded a fellowship under the TCS PhD Research Scholar Program.

<!--more-->
Tata Consultancy Services have devised the TCS Research Scholar Program to improve the research talent base in the country to perform research in areas ranging from computer engineering and science, information systems and technology, and software engineering and other disciplines which utilize computing. Shiva has been selected as a TCS PhD Research Scholar for the 2025 cycle.

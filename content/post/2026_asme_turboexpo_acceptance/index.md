Aritra's manuscript on LES of trapped vortex combustors went through peer review and got accepted for presentation at ASME Turbo Expo to be held in Milan, Italy in June 2026.

<!--more-->
The Turbomachinery Technical Conference & Exposition is the ASME's annual flagship event. A manuscript titled "Large-eddy simulations of an ammonia-fired trapped vortex combustor" authored by Aritra Roy Choudhury and Konduri Aditya has been accepted for presentation at the conference.

FLAME lab members attended the 26th Annual CFD symposium organized by Aeronautical Society of India (AeSI) CFD Division, held in HAL Management Academy, Bangalore from August 11 to 13, 2025. 

<!--more-->
The AeSI CFD symposium is a national forum for CFD researchers, developers, and practitioners, providing an opportunity to review the current state of the art in CFD and its applications, and to exchange ideas among CFD researchers in India. Aritra, Shiva, Sudhakar and Aswin provided oral presentations of their works in the symposium.

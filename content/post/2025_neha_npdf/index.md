Neha Yedala has been awarded the prestigious National Post Doctoral Fellowship (N-PDF), recognizing her strong academic and research achievements. The fellowship supports her continued contributions to advanced research and innovation in her field.


<!--more-->
Neha's research, titled “Investigation of Ammonia–Hydrogen Auto-Ignition Flames in Sequential Combustor Using Machine Learning-Enhanced Large-Eddy Simulations,” focuses on understanding the complex combustion characteristics of ammonia–hydrogen fuel blends in advanced combustor systems. By integrating high-fidelity Large-Eddy Simulations with machine learning techniques, the work aims to improve predictive capabilities, enhance combustion efficiency, and support the development of cleaner and more sustainable energy technologies.

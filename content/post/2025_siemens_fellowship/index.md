N. Surya (Ph.D.), Aswin Kumar A, and Surya Datta Sudhakar (M.Tech Research) have been awarded with IISc-Siemens Technology India Fellowships.

<!--more-->
Siemens Limited is a leading technology company in India, delivering solutions across industry, infrastructure, digital transformation, transport, and power to drive efficiency and sustainable growth. Aswin Kumar A and Surya Datta Sudhakar (M.Tech Research), along with N Surya (Ph.D.), have been selected for the IISc-Siemens Technology India Fellowships, recognizing their academic excellence and contributions to advanced research on fast solvers for reacting flows.

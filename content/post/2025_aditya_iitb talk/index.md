Dr. Aditya delivered an invited talk at the Indo-French Workshop on Hydrogen and Ammonia for Green Energy Transition held at IIT Bombay.


<!--more-->
The workshop, titled “Advancements in Hydrogen and Ammonia for Green Energy Transition,” brought together researchers from India and France to discuss developments in hydrogen- and ammonia-based decarbonized combustion.Dr. Aditya presented a talk on “Auto-ignitive Flames in Hydrogen-Fired Reheat Burner: Combustion Physics and Flashback Control.”

Dr. Aditya delivered a talk on Machine Learning for Combustion at the International Symposium on AI-Driven CFD 2026 held at IIT Gandhinagar, presenting recent advances in the use of data-driven approaches for combustion research.


<!--more-->
At the same event, Nithin secured first place for his poster titled “Chemical Mechanism Reduction for Combustion Applications Using Graph Neural Networks,” while Sudhakar secured second place for his poster titled “Transfer Learning for Machine Learning-Based Subgrid-Scale Prediction in LES of Turbulent Flow.”
Dr. Aditya, along with Nithin and Sudhakar, represented the department at the symposium.

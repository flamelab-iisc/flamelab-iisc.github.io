Dr. Aditya delivered an invited talk at the International Conference on Advanced Scientific Computing and Machine Learning (ASCML 2026) held at BITS Pilani,Goa.


<!--more-->
ASCML 2026, hosted by the Department of Mathematics at BITS Pilani, K.K. Birla Goa Campus, brought together researchers in advanced scientific computing and machine learning. Dr. Aditya delivered a talk on “Scalable Methods for Flow Solvers: Application to Combustion,” highlighting recent advances in computational approaches for complex flow and combustion systems.

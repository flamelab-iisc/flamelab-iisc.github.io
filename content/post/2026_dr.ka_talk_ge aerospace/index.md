Dr. Aditya delivered a talk at GE Aerospace, Bengaluru on Machine Learning for Combustion, highlighting the role of data-driven methods in advancing combustion research.



<!--more-->
GE Aerospace is a leading global aerospace company known for its work in aircraft engines, propulsion technologies, and advanced engineering solutions. The talk focused on the application of machine learning techniques to model complex combustion processes, improve predictive accuracy, and analyze high-dimensional data. It also provided an opportunity to share ongoing research insights and explore potential applications at the intersection of data science and aerospace engineering.

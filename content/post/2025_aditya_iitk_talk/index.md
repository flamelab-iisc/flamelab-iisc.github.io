Dr. Aditya was invited to deliver a talk, contributing to technical discussions and collaborative sessions aimed at fostering research exchange and long-term academic partnerships between the two countries.

<!--more-->
The workshop, organized by the Department of Aerospace Engineering at IIT Kanpur in collaboration with leading Japanese institutions, brought together researchers from India and Japan to discuss recent advances at the intersection of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and High-Performance Computing (HPC).
